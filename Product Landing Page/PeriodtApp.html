Welcome to all things Periods

Join us to put an end to period poverty and stigma





Menstrual hygiene education

We aim to educate the public, debunking myths about menstruation, start conversations about menstrual hygiene and products; give professional advice on menstrual health

Advocacy for non-binary persons

We aim to ameliorate stigmatization and violence faced by transgenders, non-binary and gender non-conforming people through menstrual equity

Eco-friendly sanitary products

We advocate and provide eco-friendly sanitary products such as organic cotton re-usable pads, 100% organic tampons with plant-based applicators, reusable discs and menstrual cups e.t.c. particularly to underprivileged and internally displaced people.
