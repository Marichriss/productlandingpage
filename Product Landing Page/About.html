Our Mission


Recognizing the Human Rights Council Resolution A/HRC/39/8 on Menstrual Hygiene Management (MHM) (2018), which calls upon member states of the United Nations (UN) to address the widespread stigma and shame surrounding menstruation and menstrual hygiene, and understanding the central role of technology and innovation in this day and age, we aim to:

  • combat period poverty and stigma,

  • raise more awareness on menstrual equity, that is ,ensuring that transgender, non-binary and non-conforming people have access to menstrual hygiene products and water and sanitary hygeine (WASH) facilities, without facing physical, verbal and emotional abuse/violence.
